---

## üí° NAMING STRATEGY TIPS

1. **Keep it short** (1-2 words max)
2. **Make it memorable** (easy to say, spell, remember)
3. **Convey value** (what it does or how it makes you feel)
4. **Check domain availability** (.app, .com, .io)
5. **Test with target audience** (ask 10 people)
6. **Consider international** (works in other languages)
7. **Avoid trends** (don't use "ly" or "ify" endings)
