## üé® Visual Design

### Character Design Options

**Option 1: Abstract/Symbolic**
- Starts as simple shape ‚Üí evolves to detailed figure
- Represents "becoming" rather than specific person
- More inclusive, less prescriptive

**Option 2: Silhouette Evolution**
- Starts as shadow ‚Üí becomes detailed silhouette
- User can customize color/style
- Represents "coming into focus"

**Option 3: Minimalist Avatar**
- Simple, clean design
- Grows in complexity and detail
- Accessories unlock with achievements

**Recommendation**: Start with Option 2 (Silhouette Evolution) - most flexible and meaningful
